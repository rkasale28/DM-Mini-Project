Data Mining Strategies on Youtube Dataset

YouTube is an American online video-sharing platform. The first ever YouTube video was uploaded on 23rd April, 2005. Since, then the popularity of YouTube has increased in many folds. This can be evident from the fact that every year the number of YouTube channels is growing tremendously. As of 2019, the no. of channels on YouTube grew up to 31 million. This number is around 25% greater as compared to 2018. Along with YouTube channels, the number of YouTube users is also increasing. Therefore, the number of active users is definitely greater than 2 billion.
These YouTube channels earn the revenue based upon how many times their video has been viewed by the users. Therefore, it is the responsibility of YouTube to help content creators reach their audience
The dataset used represents the day to day list of top trending Indian YouTube videos. The dataset was collected using YouTube API. The dataset includes several days of record with up to 5000 videos. Data includes the attributes as follows:
  • title
  • channel_title
  • publish_time
  • trending_date
  • views
  • likes
  • dislikes
  • comment_count
  • comments_disabled
  • ratings_disabled
  • category_id
